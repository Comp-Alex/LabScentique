Project Management

ISO 21500 Compliance

LabScentique follows ISO 21500:2021 guidelines for project management, ensuring efficient and effective development of our fragrance products.

What is ISO 21500?

ISO 21500:2021 provides guidance for project management and can be used by any type of organization, including public, private or community organizations, and for any type of project.

At LabScentique, we apply these principles to manage our fragrance development projects, ensuring high-quality outcomes and efficient processes.

Benefits of ISO 21500

Structured Approach

Provides a high-level description of concepts and processes for good project management.

Improved Efficiency

Helps in planning, executing, and controlling fragrance development projects.

Quality Assurance

Ensures consistent quality in our product development lifecycle.

Stakeholder Satisfaction

Enhances communication and satisfaction for all project stakeholders.

Our Project Management Practices

  • Initiation and planning of fragrance development projects
  • Stakeholder identification and engagement
  • Risk management and mitigation
  • Resource allocation and scheduling
  • Quality control and assurance
  • Monitoring and control throughout the project lifecycle
